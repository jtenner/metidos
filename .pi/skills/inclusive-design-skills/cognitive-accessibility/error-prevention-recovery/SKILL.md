# Error Prevention & Recovery

Use this skill when reviewing tasks where mistakes are common or costly. Focus on helping users avoid errors, understand what went wrong, and recover without losing progress or confidence.

## Identify Risk Points

- Look for ambiguous labels, hidden constraints, and irreversible actions
- Check where users must remember rules, sequence, or formatting from earlier steps
- Flag unclear validation, late error messages, or confusing correction flows
- Consider attention lapses, stress, dyslexia, and executive function challenges

## Improve Recovery

- Prevent errors with clear examples, defaults, and inline guidance
- Validate early where possible and explain problems in simple language
- Preserve user input and provide easy ways to retry, undo, or confirm
- Distinguish destructive actions and ask for confirmation only when helpful

## Output

- Highlight likely error traps and their impact
- Recommend changes that reduce mistakes and support recovery
- Prioritize fixes for high-consequence and high-frequency workflows
