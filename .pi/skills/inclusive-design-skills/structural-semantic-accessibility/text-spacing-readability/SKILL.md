# Text Spacing & Readability

Use this skill when evaluating readability and resilient typography. Focus on whether text remains legible, scannable, and usable when users adjust spacing or zoom to meet their needs.

## Review Reading Experience

- Check line height, paragraph spacing, letter spacing, word spacing, and line length
- Identify clipping, overlap, truncation, or layout breakage under text customization
- Consider dyslexia, low vision, cognitive fatigue, and mobile reading contexts
- Review long-form content, dense settings pages, and form instructions

## Improve Readability

- Support user-adjusted spacing and browser zoom without loss of content or function
- Break up dense text with headings, lists, and shorter paragraphs
- Keep measure and alignment comfortable for sustained reading
- Avoid typography choices that reduce clarity for style alone

## Output

- Highlight readability and spacing failures
- Recommend changes that improve comfort and resilience
- Prioritize fixes for content-heavy and instruction-critical screens
