# User Research & Inclusive Testing

Use this skill when shaping research and testing practice. Focus on whether the team is learning from the people most affected by accessibility and inclusion decisions.

## Review Research Inclusion

- Check whether disabled participants and diverse contexts are represented
- Identify recruitment, compensation, or logistics barriers that may exclude participants
- Review scripts, prototypes, and moderation methods for accessibility
- Consider remote, assisted, and asynchronous participation options where helpful

## Improve Practice

- Involve participants with relevant lived experience early and repeatedly
- Make consent, scheduling, materials, and communication accessible
- Avoid treating disabled users as edge cases or last-stage validation only
- Translate findings into concrete product decisions and accountability

## Output

- Highlight gaps in participant inclusion or accessible research practice
- Recommend specific improvements to recruitment, facilitation, and synthesis
- Prioritize changes that improve the validity and equity of product decisions
