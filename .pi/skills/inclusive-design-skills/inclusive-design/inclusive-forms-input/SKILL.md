# Inclusive Forms & Input

Use this skill when evaluating form completion. Focus on making input understandable, efficient, forgiving, and accessible across devices, abilities, and confidence levels.

## Review Form Experience

- Check labels, instructions, grouping, required states, and field order
- Identify unnecessary typing, unclear formatting expectations, or inaccessible controls
- Review validation timing, error clarity, and recovery support
- Consider keyboard, screen reader, mobile, cognitive, and low-literacy needs together

## Improve Completion

- Keep labels persistent and examples specific
- Reduce input effort with sensible defaults, autofill, and simple choices
- Place errors near the relevant fields and explain how to fix them
- Preserve entered data and support review before submission

## Output

- Highlight barriers that slow or block completion
- Recommend form changes that reduce effort and increase success
- Prioritize fixes in critical workflows and high-friction steps
